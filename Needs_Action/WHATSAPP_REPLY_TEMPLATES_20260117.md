# WhatsApp Reply Templates

## Template 1: Business Inquiry Response
```
Hi there! Thanks for reaching out about [topic]. I'm reviewing your message and will get back to you shortly with a detailed response. For urgent matters, please call us at [phone number]. Looking forward to connecting!
```

## Template 2: General Greeting
```
Hello! Thank you for contacting us. We've received your message and our team will respond within 24 hours. If your inquiry is urgent, please let us know and we'll prioritize accordingly.
```

## Template 3: AI/Technology Discussion
```
Thanks for your interest in AI technology! The field is evolving rapidly with exciting developments in machine learning and automation. I'd be happy to discuss how these technologies might benefit your specific needs. What particular aspect interests you most?
```

## Template 4: Follow-up Response
```
Following up on our previous conversation, I wanted to share some additional insights/resources that might be helpful for your project. Are you available for a brief call this week to discuss further?
```

## Template 5: Meeting/Collaboration Request
```
Thanks for suggesting a meeting/collaboration opportunity. I'm interested in exploring this further. How about [suggest 2-3 time slots]? Alternatively, feel free to suggest a time that works better for your schedule.
```

---
**Instructions**: These templates can be customized based on the specific message received and moved to the Approved folder for processing.
