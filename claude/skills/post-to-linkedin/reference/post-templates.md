## Personalization Guidelines

### Variables to Customize

1. **[Client Context]** - Industry, size, specific challenge
2. **[Metric/Result]** - Quantifiable impact (hours saved, revenue gained, errors reduced)
3. **[Timeframe]** - Duration of project or result measurement period
4. **[Tool/Technology]** - Specific tech stack or methodology
5. **[Learning/Insight]** - Key takeaway or lesson learned
6. **[Your Differentiator]** - What makes your approach unique

### Brand Voice Integration

From Company_Handbook.md (social media rules), maintain:
- **Professional but not stiff** - Conversational yet credible
- **Expert without arrogance** - Share knowledge humbly
- **Helpful and genuine** - Focus on providing value
- **Transparent and authentic** - Real experiences, honest insights

### Quality Checklist (Apply to All Templates)

Before using any template, ensure:

- [ ] **Hook:** First sentence grabs attention (question, stat, or bold statement)
- [ ] **Value:** Post provides insight, lesson, or useful information
- [ ] **Clarity:** Easy to scan (short paragraphs, bullets, emojis for visual breaks)
- [ ] **Authenticity:** Sounds like you (not generic corporate speak)
- [ ] **Length:** 150-300 words optimal (LinkedIn sweet spot)
- [ ] **CTA:** Ends with question, invitation, or next step
- [ ] **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant tags (not excessive)
- [ ] **Proofreading:** Zero typos or grammar errors

## Advanced Tips

### Engagement Optimization

1. **Timing:** Post Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM or 4-6 PM (highest engagement)
2. **Line Breaks:** Every 2-3 sentences for mobile readability
3. **Emoji Usage:** 1-2 professional emojis max (üéØ‚úÖüöÄüí°üìä)
4. **Questions:** End with a question to boost comments
5. **Tagging:** Mention relevant people/companies (with permission)

### Content Recycling

- Repurpose project completion updates ‚Üí client success stories (3 months later)
- Turn client questions (FAQ) ‚Üí industry insight posts
- Series approach: "3-part series on automation" (builds anticipation)

### Seasonal Content

- Q1: Year planning, goal-setting, fresh starts
- Q2: Mid-year reviews, lessons learned
- Q3: Preparing for Q4 push, productivity hacks
- Q4: Year-in-review, achievements, gratitude
