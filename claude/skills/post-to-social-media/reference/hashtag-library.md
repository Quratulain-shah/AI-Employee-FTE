## Purpose

A curated hashtag library organized by industry, size, and platform. Use this reference to quickly select relevant hashtags for Facebook (1-2) and Instagram (5-10) posts.

---

## Hashtag Size Classification

### Understanding Hashtag Sizes

**Niche (10K - 100K posts):**
- Best for: Targeted reach, higher ranking potential
- Competition: Low to medium
- Conversion: Higher (specific audience)
- Use: 3-4 per Instagram post

**Medium (100K - 500K posts):**
- Best for: Balance of reach and targeting
- Competition: Medium
- Conversion: Moderate
- Use: 3-4 per Instagram post

**Popular (1M+ posts):**
- Best for: Maximum reach, viral potential
- Competition: Very high
- Conversion: Lower (broad audience)
- Use: 1-2 per Instagram post

**Branded (Your own):**
- Best for: Building brand identity, tracking UGC
- Competition: None (yours)
- Conversion: Highest (your community)
- Use: 1 per post always

---

## Platform-Specific Strategy

### Facebook Hashtag Usage
- **Recommended:** 0-2 hashtags maximum
- **Performance:** Minimal impact on reach
- **When to use:** Branded hashtag, event/campaign hashtag
- **Placement:** End of post
- **Note:** Facebook algorithm prioritizes content quality over hashtags

### Instagram Hashtag Usage
- **Recommended:** 5-10 hashtags (sweet spot)
- **Maximum:** 30 (but diminishing returns after 10)
- **Performance:** Critical for discovery and reach
- **Placement:** Caption or first comment (both work)
- **Mix:** Combine niche + medium + popular sizes

## Hashtag Rotation Strategy

### Don't Use Same Set Every Time
Instagram may flag as spam if you use identical hashtag sets repeatedly.

**Solution: Create 5-7 Rotating Sets**

**Example: Automation Business**

**Set A (Mon):** AI + Productivity focus
```
#AIAutomation #ProductivityTools #ClaudeCode
#ProductivityHacks #WorkSmart #AITools
#Productivity #AI #YourBrand
```

**Set B (Wed):** Business + Entrepreneur focus
```
#SmallBusinessOwner #SolopreneurLife #WorkflowAutomation
#EntrepreneurLife #BusinessOwner #AutomationLife
#SmallBusiness #Entrepreneur #YourBrand
```

**Set C (Fri):** Tech + Innovation focus
```
#TechForBusiness #AutomationTools #SmartAutomation
#TechLife #ProductivityTips #DigitalTools
#Technology #Innovation #YourBrand
```

Rotate through sets to avoid repetition flags.

## Hashtag Best Practices Summary

### DO:
✅ Mix niche, medium, and popular sizes
✅ Always include your branded hashtag
✅ Research hashtags before using (check relevance)
✅ Rotate hashtag sets to avoid spam flags
✅ Update library quarterly based on performance
✅ Use 5-10 for Instagram, 0-2 for Facebook

### DON'T:
❌ Use 30 hashtags (looks desperate)
❌ Use irrelevant popular tags for reach (hurts authenticity)
❌ Copy-paste same hashtags every post
❌ Use banned or spammy hashtags (#like4like, #follow4follow)
❌ Use hashtags with millions of posts only (too competitive)
❌ Ignore hashtag performance data

1. Pick 3 niche hashtags (10K-100K)

4. Add 1 branded hashtag (yours)

**For Facebook:**

2. Optional: Pick 1 campaign/event hashtag
