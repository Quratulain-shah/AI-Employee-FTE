## Facebook Platform Characteristics

### Audience Profile
- **Demographics:** Broad age range (25-65+), family-oriented
- **Behavior:** Community engagement, groups, event discovery
- **Mindset:** Social connection, information sharing, discussions
- **Best Use:** Community building, local business, longer storytelling

### Content Performance Insights
- **Text Posts:** Perform well if conversation-starting
- **Image Posts:** 2.3x more engagement than text-only
- **Video Posts:** 5x more engagement (native video preferred over links)
- **Link Posts:** Lower reach (algorithm de-prioritizes external links)

---

## Facebook Best Practices

### Optimal Post Length
- **Sweet Spot:** 40-80 words
- **Acceptable Range:** 100-250 words for storytelling
- **Limit:** 63,206 characters (but brevity wins)

**Why?** Facebook users scroll fast. Concise posts with clear value get more engagement.

### Hashtag Usage
- **Recommended:** 0-2 hashtags
- **Unlike Instagram:** Hashtags don't significantly boost reach
- **When to Use:** Branded hashtags, event hashtags, trending topics

**Facebook's Algorithm Prioritizes:**
1. Meaningful interactions (comments, shares)
2. Friends and family content
3. Video content (especially live)
4. Recency (post timing matters)

## Writing Style Guidelines

### Voice & Tone
- **Conversational:** Write like you're talking to a friend
- **Inclusive:** Use "we" and "our" to build community
- **Authentic:** Share real experiences, not just wins
- **Helpful:** Focus on value for the reader

### Formatting for Readability
```
Use line breaks every 2-3 sentences.

This makes posts easier to scan.

Mobile users (80% of Facebook) especially appreciate white space.
```

### Emoji Usage
- **Amount:** 1-3 per post maximum
- **Placement:** Usually at end of sentences or as bullet points
- **Purpose:** Add personality, not distraction
- **Acceptable:** ‚úÖ üí° üöÄ üéâ üí¨ üì¢ üëá ‚ö° üôè
- **Avoid:** Overuse, unprofessional emojis, emoji spam

### Calls-to-Action (CTAs)
**Strong CTAs:**
- "What's your experience? Share below!"
- "Drop a comment if you want to see our setup"
- "Tag a friend who needs to hear this"
- "Save this for later when you're ready to automate"

**Weak CTAs:**
- "Like if you agree" (low effort, doesn't boost reach)
- "Click the link" (algorithm penalties)
- No CTA at all

## Engagement Optimization

### Best Posting Times
Based on business/professional audience:
- **Peak Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Peak Times:** 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM (lunch/afternoon break)
- **Weekend Sweet Spot:** Saturday 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM
- **Avoid:** Early mornings (< 9 AM), late evenings (> 9 PM)

### Boosting Engagement
1. **Ask Questions:** Posts with questions get 100% more comments
2. **Respond Fast:** Reply to comments within first hour
3. **Tag Relevant People:** When appropriate (not spammy)
4. **Use Native Features:** Facebook Live, Stories get priority
5. **Encourage Shares:** "Tag someone who..." performs well

### What to Avoid
- ‚ùå Clickbait headlines
- ‚ùå Engagement bait ("Like for X, Comment for Y")
- ‚ùå Too many hashtags (looks spammy)
- ‚ùå External links in main text (use first comment instead)
- ‚ùå Controversial/political content (unless brand-aligned)
- ‚ùå All-caps text (looks aggressive)

## Content Mix Recommendation

**Monthly Content Distribution:**
- 40% Value/Educational (tips, insights, how-tos)
- 30% Community/Engagement (questions, discussions)
- 20% Behind-the-Scenes (process, culture, authenticity)
- 10% Promotional (services, offers, announcements)

**Posting Frequency:**
- **Minimum:** 2x per week (maintain presence)
- **Optimal:** 3-5x per week (balanced consistency)
- **Maximum:** 7x per week (daily, but risks fatigue)

## Image Specifications

### Recommended Sizes
- **Link Post Image:** 1200 x 630 px
- **Photo Post:** 1200 x 1200 px (square) or 1200 x 1500 px (portrait)
- **Cover Photo:** 820 x 312 px
- **Profile Picture:** 180 x 180 px (displays as circle)

### Image Best Practices
- **Text Overlay:** Keep to < 20% of image (Facebook guideline)
- **Resolution:** High quality, but < 1 MB file size
- **Brand Consistency:** Use consistent colors, fonts, style
- **Mobile-First:** Most views are mobile - test on small screens

## Facebook-Specific Features

### Facebook Stories (24-hour content)
- **Use Case:** Behind-the-scenes, timely updates, polls
- **Specs:** 1080 x 1920 px (9:16 ratio)
- **Engagement:** Swipe-up links (for Pages with 10K+ followers)

### Facebook Live
- **Best Performance:** 3-10 minute live videos
- **Use Case:** Q&As, product demos, event coverage
- **Pro Tip:** Announce live sessions 24h in advance

### Facebook Groups
- **Strategy:** Join relevant industry groups
- **Engagement:** Provide value before promoting
- **Etiquette:** Follow group rules, avoid spamming

## Compliance & Safety

### What NOT to Post
- Confidential client information
- Unverified claims or statistics
- Copyrighted images (without license)
- Health/medical advice (unless qualified)
- Financial advice (unless qualified)
- Political endorsements (usually)

### Required Disclosures
- Paid partnerships: Use "Paid partnership with" tag
- Affiliate links: Disclose in post
- Sponsored content: Must use Facebook's branded content tools

## Analytics to Track

**Key Metrics:**
1. **Reach:** How many people saw your post
2. **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Reach
3. **Click-Through Rate:** If post has link
4. **Shares:** Best indicator of valuable content
5. **Comments:** Depth of engagement

**Benchmarks:**
- Engagement Rate: 1-3% is average, 5%+ is excellent
- Video Completion Rate: 50%+ is good
- Share Rate: 0.5% of reach is strong
