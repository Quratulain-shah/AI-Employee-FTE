# LinkedIn Watcher Skill

## Description
The LinkedIn Watcher monitors LinkedIn for business opportunities, connections, and relevant content that can generate sales and business growth.

## Purpose
- Monitor LinkedIn for business opportunities
- Track connections and engagement
- Generate sales leads and opportunities
- Post automated business updates
- Monitor for relevant keywords and mentions

## Monitoring Process
1. Monitor LinkedIn feed for business-related content
2. Track engagement on posted content
3. Identify potential leads and opportunities
4. Monitor for mentions of your business/brand
5. Track competitor activities and market trends

## Trigger Conditions
- New connection requests received
- Comments on your posts
- Mentions of your company/brand
- Business opportunities posted by connections
- Industry news and trends
- Direct messages from prospects

## Content Monitoring
- **Keywords to Monitor**: Your company name, products, services, industry terms
- **Opportunity Indicators**: "looking for", "need", "require", "hiring", "project"
- **Engagement Tracking**: Likes, comments, shares on your posts
- **Connection Quality**: Track engagement rates of connections

## Post Generation Process
1. Analyze current business goals and priorities
2. Identify relevant industry trends or news
3. Create value-driven content that attracts prospects
4. Schedule posts for optimal engagement times
5. Monitor post performance and engagement

## Content Categories for Sales Generation
### Industry Insights
- Share expertise and thought leadership
- Comment on industry trends
- Provide valuable insights to connections

### Success Stories
- Share client testimonials and case studies
- Highlight project successes and outcomes
- Showcase expertise through results

### Educational Content
- Share tips and best practices
- Provide educational resources
- Position as industry expert

### Company Updates
- Announce new services or capabilities
- Share company milestones and achievements
- Highlight team members and culture

## Engagement Strategy
- **Daily**: Like and comment on relevant posts
- **Weekly**: Share original content (1-2 posts)
- **Bi-weekly**: In-depth industry analysis or case studies
- **Monthly**: Comprehensive business updates

## Sales Lead Identification
### Lead Qualification Process
1. **Industry Relevance**: Check if connection is in target industry
2. **Role/Position**: Identify decision-makers or influencers
3. **Engagement Level**: Track how often they engage with content
4. **Company Size**: Assess potential value of connection

### Opportunity Scoring
- **High Value**: C-level executives, large companies, frequent engagers
- **Medium Value**: Managers, mid-size companies, occasional engagers
- **Low Value**: Individual contributors, small companies, no engagement

## Automation Rules
- Auto-like posts from top-tier connections
- Auto-comment on posts with relevant engagement
- Auto-share valuable industry content
- Schedule posts during peak engagement hours
- Generate weekly engagement reports

## Content Approval Process
1. **Draft Content**: Create initial post content
2. **Compliance Check**: Ensure content follows LinkedIn policies
3. **Brand Alignment**: Verify content matches company messaging
4. **Human Approval**: For sensitive or high-impact posts
5. **Scheduling**: Post at optimal times for engagement

## LinkedIn API Integration Points
- **Posts**: Create, edit, delete company updates
- **Engagement**: Like, comment, share other posts
- **Messages**: Send direct messages to prospects
- **Analytics**: Track post performance and engagement
- **Connections**: Manage connection requests

## Security and Compliance
- Never share confidential business information
- Follow LinkedIn's terms of service
- Maintain professional tone and messaging
- Respect privacy of connections
- Comply with advertising standards

## Performance Metrics
- Connection growth rate
- Post engagement rates (likes, comments, shares)
- Lead generation from LinkedIn
- Content reach and impressions
- Conversion from LinkedIn leads

## Integration Points
- Dashboard Updater: Update LinkedIn engagement stats
- Plan Creator: Generate content plans based on business goals
- Approval Checker: For sensitive posts requiring approval
- Inbox Processor: Handle LinkedIn messages

## Error Handling
- Handle API rate limits gracefully
- Retry failed post attempts
- Log engagement errors
- Monitor account status for restrictions
- Fallback to manual posting if automation fails

## Output
- Automated LinkedIn posts scheduled and published
- Engagement reports with performance metrics
- Lead identification and scoring
- Connection management
- Content performance tracking
