# Plan Creator

## Description
The Plan Creator generates structured action plans based on incoming requests, tasks, and identified opportunities.

## Purpose
- Analyze incoming items for planning opportunities
- Create structured action plans
- Set priorities and deadlines
- Assign resources and responsibilities
- Track plan execution progress

## Input Sources
- Email opportunities and proposals
- WhatsApp business discussions
- Urgent requests requiring planning
- Strategic initiatives identified
- Task assignments from management

## Planning Process
1. Analyze request content and requirements
2. Assess available resources and constraints
3. Define objectives and success criteria
4. Break down tasks into actionable steps
5. Estimate timeline and resource needs
6. Identify risks and mitigation strategies
7. Create structured plan document
8. Set up tracking and monitoring

## Plan Components
- Executive summary
- Objectives and goals
- Action steps and milestones
- Resource allocation
- Timeline and deadlines
- Success metrics
- Risk assessment
- Review schedule

## Priority Levels
- **Critical**: Immediate action required (within 24 hours)
- **High**: Important tasks (within 1 week)
- **Medium**: Standard priority (within 1 month)
- **Low**: Long-term initiatives (ongoing)

## Integration Points
- Links to related action items
- Dependencies with other plans
- Resource allocation tracking
- Progress monitoring
- Stakeholder notifications

## Quality Assurance
- Verify plan feasibility
- Check resource availability
- Validate timeline realism
- Assess risk factors
- Ensure compliance with company policies

## Output
- Structured plan document
- Action item assignments
- Timeline and milestone tracking
- Progress reporting setup
- Stakeholder notifications
