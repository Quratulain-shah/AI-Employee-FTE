## Purpose

Strategic guidelines for maximizing engagement on Facebook and Instagram through timing, interaction tactics, and algorithm optimization. This document covers both organic growth strategies and tactical engagement best practices.

## Platform Algorithms Overview

### Facebook Algorithm Priorities (2026)
1. **Meaningful Interactions:** Comments and shares > likes
2. **Friends & Family First:** Personal connections outrank business pages
3. **Video Content:** Native video (especially Live) gets 5x reach
4. **Recency:** Fresh content prioritized
5. **Time Spent:** Content that keeps users on platform longer

**Implication:** Facebook posts must spark genuine conversations to gain reach.

### Instagram Algorithm Priorities (2026)
1. **Relationship:** Accounts user engages with regularly
2. **Interest:** Content similar to what user usually likes
3. **Timeliness:** Recency matters (first hour critical)
4. **Frequency:** How often user opens Instagram
5. **Session Time:** Longer engagement = better ranking
6. **Profile Searches:** If user searches for you directly

**Implication:** Early engagement in first hour determines post reach. Consistent posting + audience interaction = algorithm favor.

## The Golden Hour: First 60 Minutes

### Why It Matters
Both platforms measure engagement velocity in the first hour to determine if content is "worthy" of broader reach.

**High First-Hour Engagement =**
- Shown to more of your followers
- Pushed to Explore page (Instagram)
- Suggested to non-followers
- Higher ranking in feeds

**Low First-Hour Engagement =**
- Limited to small portion of followers
- No Explore page placement
- Quickly buried in feeds

### First-Hour Optimization Tactics

**Before Posting:**
- Post at your optimal times (see timing section below)
- Ensure high-quality content (avoid rushed posts)
- Have engaging caption with clear CTA

**Within 5 Minutes:**
- Reply to first 3 comments immediately
- Like every comment
- Ask follow-up questions in replies

**Within 15 Minutes:**
- Engage with 5-10 posts in your niche (likes + comments)
- Check for DMs related to post, respond
- Share post to your Story (if Instagram)

**Within 60 Minutes:**
- Reply to all new comments
- Continue engaging with niche accounts
- Monitor analytics (Insights)

**Pro Tip:** Block calendar for 30 min after posting to manage first-hour engagement.

### Engagement Hierarchy (Value Per Action)

**Instagram:**
1. **Saves (Highest):** User bookmarks for later = high-value signal
2. **Shares:** User sends to friend or shares to Story
3. **Comments:** Written interaction (especially long comments)
4. **Likes:** Basic interaction (lowest value but still counts)

**Facebook:**
1. **Shares (Highest):** Spreading content to others
2. **Comments:** Written interaction, especially conversations
3. **Reactions:** Different reactions (Love, Haha, Wow) > Like
4. **Likes:** Basic interaction (lowest value)

**Strategy:** Design posts to elicit saves (Instagram) and shares (Facebook), not just likes.

## Optimal Posting Times (Data-Backed)

### Facebook Best Times

**Weekday Optimal:**
- **Best Days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times:** 1:00 PM - 4:00 PM (lunch and afternoon breaks)
- **Secondary:** 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM (morning check-ins)

**Weekend:**
- **Saturday:** 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM (lunch hour)
- **Sunday:** Low engagement (skip or use Stories only)

**Worst Times:**
- Before 9:00 AM (people commuting, not engaging)
- After 8:00 PM weeknights (personal time)
- All day Sunday

### Instagram Best Times

**Weekday Optimal:**
- **Best Days:** Monday, Wednesday, Friday
- **Best Times:**
  - 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM (lunch scroll)
  - 7:00 PM - 9:00 PM (evening wind-down)

**Weekend:**
- **Saturday:** 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM

**Worst Times:**
- Before 8:00 AM (except fitness/wellness niches)
- 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM (work hours, low engagement)
- After 10:00 PM (people sleeping)

**Important:** These are baseline averages. Check YOUR audience insights in Facebook/Instagram analytics to refine for your specific followers.

## Posting Frequency Impact

### Facebook Posting Frequency

**Under-Posting (< 2x/week):**
- Algorithm de-prioritizes infrequent pages
- Audience forgets about you
- Growth stalls

**Optimal (3-5x/week):**
- Consistent visibility
- Algorithm favor
- Steady growth

**Over-Posting (> 7x/week):**
- Audience fatigue
- Lower per-post engagement
- Unfollows increase

**Recommendation:** 3-5 posts per week, evenly distributed

### Instagram Posting Frequency

**Under-Posting (< 3x/week):**
- Algorithm de-prioritizes
- Lose momentum
- Followers forget

**Optimal (5-7x/week / Daily):**
- Daily visibility in feeds
- Algorithm boost
- Consistent growth

**Over-Posting (> 2x/day consistently):**
- Audience fatigue possible
- Quality may suffer
- Diminishing returns

**Recommendation:** 5-7 posts per week (aim for daily) + daily Stories

## Growth Tactics (Organic)

- High-quality visuals (Instagram especially)

**7. Cross-Platform Promotion**
- Promote Instagram on Facebook

## Analytics to Track Weekly

### Facebook Metrics (Facebook Insights)
- **Reach:** How many unique users saw content
- **Engagement Rate:** (Reactions + Comments + Shares) / Reach Ã— 100
- **Post Clicks:** Link clicks, profile views
- **Top Posts:** Identify patterns in high performers
- **Peak Online Times:** When your audience is most active

### Instagram Metrics (Instagram Insights)
- **Reach:** Unique accounts reached
- **Impressions:** Total views
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Saves + Shares) / Reach Ã— 100
- **Saves:** High-value content indicator
- **Profile Visits:** From specific posts
- **Website Clicks:** If you have link
- **Follower Growth:** Net followers gained/lost

### Weekly Review Process (15 minutes, Friday)

1. **Identify Top 3 Performing Posts**
   - What content type?
   - What time posted?
   - What made it successful?

2. **Identify Lowest Performer**
   - Why did it flop?
   - Wrong time?
   - Weak hook?
   - Avoid this pattern

3. **Check Follower Growth**
   - Net gain/loss this week
   - Any sudden drops? (check if you lost followers after specific post)

4. **Adjust Next Week's Strategy**
   - Double down on what worked
   - Fix what didn't
   - Test one new content type

## Engagement Red Flags (What to Avoid)

### Shadow Ban Triggers (Instagram)
- Using banned hashtags (check before using)
- Posting same hashtags repeatedly
- Excessive automation (bots)
- Aggressive follow/unfollow tactics
- Reported content (controversial, spammy)

**How to Check:** Search your recent hashtags logged out. If your post doesn't appear, potential shadow ban.

**Recovery:** Stop using flagged hashtags, engage authentically for 7-14 days, avoid automation tools.

### Algorithm Penalties (Both Platforms)
- Sudden engagement drop after posting = potential penalty
- Causes: Clickbait, engagement bait, policy violations
- Recovery: Return to authentic, value-driven content
